Frequently Asked Questions

How does blockchain ensure document security? +

Blockchain stores document hashes in a decentralized ledger. Once recorded, the data cannot be altered, making documents tamper-proof and highly secure.

Is public verification available without login? +

Yes. Public users can verify documents using a verification ID or QR code without logging in.

How accurate is AI forgery detection? +

AI forgery detection uses pattern recognition and anomaly detection techniques to identify tampering and fake documents with high accuracy.

Who can access verification and audit logs? +

Audit and verification logs are accessible only to authorized administrators and institutions.
